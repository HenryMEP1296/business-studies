Our Services

A cut above is a garden care business. We focus on high standard garden and pavement transformations. We also focus on quality at an affordable price and a trustworthy and local business that you can rely on to do a nice job of your garden. Our vision statement is that we want to become a trustworthy local business that can be your regular Our Mission statement is: To give garden owners an affordable way to transform their garden and to assist those who can't take care of their own garden .

Here at a cut above we can offer four aspects of gardencare which are Leaf blowing, Water blasting, Lawn mowing, Weeding and trimming hedges and you can see the prices for these on the Book Gardencare section of this page. For each service we can ensure that each job will be done to the best quality possible and with the right tools.
